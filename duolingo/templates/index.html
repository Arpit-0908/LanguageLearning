How to Become a Teacher

Join our community of educators and inspire the next generation of learners. Here's what you need to become a teacher on our platform:

1. Certification Requirement

To apply, you must provide proof of certification or demonstrate extensive experience in the language you wish to teach. Preference is given to certifications from accredited institutions or well-regarded language programs.

2. Teaching Experience

Applicants should have at least 2 years of teaching experience, whether in-person or online. Previous online teaching experience is a plus as it shows familiarity with virtual learning environments.

3. Availability

You must be available for at least 10 hours a week to conduct lessons and interact with students. Flexibility in scheduling to cater to various time zones is highly recommended.

4. Language Proficiency

To ensure quality education, all prospective teachers must pass a proficiency exam in the language they wish to teach. Native or near-native speakers are preferred for certain languages.

5. Professional Conduct

Teachers are expected to maintain a high level of professionalism and courtesy. This includes providing timely feedback, maintaining respect during classes, and supporting students effectively.

6. Equipment Requirements

You need a reliable computer, a high-speed internet connection, and appropriate audio/visual equipment to provide seamless online lessons. A quiet, well-lit, and distraction-free environment is also crucial.
